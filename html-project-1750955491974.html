A Different Kind of Classroom

An Akiak education is defined by action. We believe the most powerful lessons happen when you apply knowledge to real-world challenges.

The First-Year Expedition

Every Akiak student begins their journey with a one-week, faculty-led expedition, setting the stage for a hands-on education.

Research from Day One

You don't have to wait to make an impact. Our undergraduates are integral members of world-class research teams.

The Capstone Project

All seniors complete a capstone project, tackling a real-world problem for a community partner or corporation.
