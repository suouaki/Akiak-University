Frequently Asked Questions

What are you looking for in an applicant?

Beyond grades and test scores, we look for curiosity, resilience, and a passion for exploration. We want students who aren't afraid of a challenge and who are eager to contribute to our unique community. Tell us what makes you tick, what adventures you've had (big or small), and what problems you want to solve.

Is Akiak University test-optional?

Yes, Akiak University is proud to be a test-optional institution. We believe a student is more than a single test score. We will holistically review your academic record, your essays, your letters of recommendation, and your extracurricular involvement to assess your potential for success.

What is financial aid like at Akiak?

We are committed to making an Akiak education accessible. Over 85% of our students receive some form of financial aid. We offer a combination of need-based grants, merit scholarships, and work-study opportunities. All applicants are automatically considered for merit scholarships, and we encourage everyone to file the FAFSA to determine their eligibility for federal and institutional aid.

Can I visit the campus?

Absolutely! We believe the best way to know if Akiak is right for you is to experience it for yourself. We offer guided on-campus tours on weekdays, as well as special "Explorer Day" open house events. For those who can't travel to Alaska, we also provide comprehensive virtual tours and online information sessions with our admissions counselors. You can schedule your visit on our website.
