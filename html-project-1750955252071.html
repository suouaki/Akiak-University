Research at the Apex

We don't just study the world's most pressing challenges. We go to where they are happening—from the arctic ice sheets to the frontiers of AI—to build the solutions for a better, more resilient future.

Pillars of Research

Our research is focused on four interdisciplinary areas where our unique location and expertise allow us to make a global impact.

Climate & Polar Systems

Decoding the past and modeling the future of Earth's critical systems, from deep oceans to high-altitude glaciers.

Resilient Engineering & Technology

Designing the materials, infrastructure, and autonomous systems needed to thrive in extreme environments on Earth and beyond.

Health & Community Wellness

Focusing on the unique health challenges and opportunities in rural, northern, and indigenous communities.

Data Science & Computation

Harnessing the power of artificial intelligence and high-performance computing to analyze complex data and model outcomes.

Featured Breakthrough

The Permafrost Paradox

Akiak University's Geo-Metastability Lab recently published a landmark paper in *Nature Geoscience*, revealing how microbial activity within melting permafrost could create a powerful carbon sink—a discovery that could reshape climate models. This $25M project utilizes autonomous drones and subterranean sensors to build the most detailed map of the arctic underground ever created.

The findings suggest that under specific conditions, certain methanotrophic bacteria thrive in thawing soil, consuming more methane—a potent greenhouse gas—than the surrounding area releases. This "paradox" challenges previous assumptions and opens new avenues for climate change mitigation strategies.

Research Centers & Institutes

Our dedicated centers and institutes serve as hubs for interdisciplinary collaboration, bringing together top minds to tackle specific challenges.

Institute for Planetary Habitability

Explores the conditions for life in extreme environments on Earth and applies them to the search for life beyond our planet.

Center for Cold Climate Health Research

Focuses on the physiological and psychological aspects of human health in northern and high-altitude populations.

Advanced Materials & Robotics Lab

Develops novel materials and autonomous systems capable of operating in the world's most demanding conditions.

Center for Indigenous Data Sovereignty

Partners with indigenous communities to develop ethical frameworks and technologies for the stewardship of cultural data.
