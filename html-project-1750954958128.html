Your Home on the Frontier

Life at Akiak is more than an education—it's an adventure in community, exploration, and self-discovery. Find your people, your passion, and your place in the world.

Live at Akiak

From modern residence halls with stunning mountain views to focused Living-Learning Communities, your home at Akiak is designed for comfort, connection, and collaboration.

Housing Options

Our two main residence halls, Kincaid Hall and Denali Commons, offer single and double rooms, state-of-the-art study lounges, and community kitchens. First-year students can also join Living-Learning Communities focused on Outdoor Leadership or Undergraduate Research.

Explore Housing

Dine at Akiak

Fuel your adventures with delicious and nourishing food. Our dining program prioritizes locally-sourced and sustainable ingredients whenever possible.

Dining Venues

The Base Camp, our main dining hall, offers all-you-can-eat options with rotating menus. For a quick bite or coffee, visit The Grind Cafe in the library or The Northern Lights Grill in the student union.

View Meal Plans

Find Your Community

With over 80 student-run clubs and organizations, your community is waiting. Whether you want to scale a mountain or a new skill, you'll find your people here.

Outdoor & Adventure

Join the Backcountry Skiing Club, the Kayaking Crew, or the Ice Climbing Collective. The wilderness is your playground.

Academic & Professional

Engage with our Engineering Society, Pre-Med Association, or the Student Business Guild to build your skills and network.

Arts & Culture

From the Digital Storytellers Guild to the A Capella group "The Northern Lights," express your creative side.

The Akiak Advantage

Adventure Awaits

Most universities have a quad. We have 1.2 million acres of national park as our backyard. Our unique location means that world-class recreation is not a vacation—it’s your weekend.

  • Guided Ice Climbing on Matanuska Glacier
  • Backcountry Skiing & Avalanche Safety Courses
  • Northern Lights Photography Workshops
  • Sea Kayaking Expeditions in Prince William Sound
  • Dog Sledding Team & Mushing Classes

Thrive at Akiak

A strong mind needs a strong body and a supportive community. We provide comprehensive resources to keep you healthy, balanced, and thriving during your time here.

Health Services

Our on-campus clinic provides medical care, wellness check-ups, and health education for all students.

Counseling Services

Confidential mental health support, including individual counseling and group therapy, is available to all students free of charge.

Basecamp Rec Center

Stay active at our state-of-the-art recreation center, featuring a fitness center, swimming pool, and a 50-foot climbing wall.
