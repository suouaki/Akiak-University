Education on the Edge of Discovery

Our curriculum is as dynamic as the landscape that surrounds us. At Akiak, we don't just teach subjects—we inspire interdisciplinary quests for knowledge that address the challenges of our time.

Our Colleges & Schools

Akiak University is organized into five distinct colleges and schools, each serving as a launchpad for intellectual exploration and professional growth.

College of Arctic & Environmental Sciences

Study climate change, marine biology, and geology in a living laboratory. Our premier programs prepare you to tackle the planet's most pressing environmental issues.

Explore Sciences

College of Engineering & Technology

Build resilient systems for extreme environments. From arctic infrastructure to aerospace technology, our engineers solve problems others deem impossible.

Explore Engineering

School of Business & Public Policy

Lead with integrity. Focus on sustainable economies, resource management, and logistics in a global context. Connect with industry leaders in the North.

Explore Business

College of Health & Social Sciences

Serve diverse communities with degrees in nursing, social work, and public health, focusing on rural and indigenous wellness in a changing world.

Explore Health

College of Arts & Humanities

Engage with diverse cultures, languages, and histories. Our programs in digital media, anthropology, and Indigenous studies offer unique perspectives on the human experience.

Explore Arts

Signature Programs

While all our programs offer a unique edge, these select degrees exemplify the spirit of Akiak University—bold, interdisciplinary, and deeply connected to our environment.

B.S. in Glacial Geology

Read the story of our planet written in ice. Our students conduct fieldwork on active glaciers from their first year, using ground-penetrating radar and isotopic analysis to model climate futures.

M.S. in Cold Region Engineering

Design infrastructure that lasts. This program focuses on creating sustainable and resilient structures, energy systems, and transportation networks for arctic and subarctic environments.

Ph.D. in Indigenous Studies & Data Sovereignty

A groundbreaking program that combines social science, law, and data ethics to empower indigenous communities in the digital age. Research focuses on language revitalization and cultural heritage preservation.

Beyond the Classroom

At Akiak, your most profound learning experiences will happen where theory meets practice—in the lab, in the field, and around the world.

Undergraduate Research

Don't wait until graduate school. Join a faculty-led research team from your first year and contribute to meaningful discoveries.

Field Studies & Expeditions

Our location is our greatest asset. Participate in semester-long field studies in Denali National Park or marine research voyages in Prince William Sound.

Global & Polar Programs

Extend your frontier. Study climate policy in Scandinavia or high-altitude geology through our research partnership in Central Asia.
